1. Introduction
This oil painting captures a fleeting moment in nature with a keen eye for detail. The subjects are a white butterfly on a blade of grass and dew drops glistening in the morning light. The artist portrays the tranquil beauty of micro-landscapes using powerful yet delicate brushwork. The work achieves a harmonious balance between vitality and transparency, drawing viewers into its intricate details.

2. Description
A butterfly with white wings clings to the edge of a diagonal blade of grass. The texture of its wings is palpable, enveloped in a soft glow. Transparent dew drops maintain spherical shapes, scattered like beads along the foliage. The background consists of mottled patches of deep green and bright yellow, suggesting a lush meadow. Light enters from the upper right, emphasizing the reflections in the water and the radiance of the wings.

3. Analysis
The most striking feature is the rich texture created through thick, impasto-like brushstrokes. In the dew drops, the artist reproduces transparency and weight by precisely placing sharp highlights. The composition is energized by the diagonal lines of the grass, providing visual rhythm while centering the butterfly. The color palette relies on a gradient from yellow-green to dark green, using the white butterfly as a focal point to achieve harmony and contrast.

5. Conclusion
The painting successfully depicts the minute drama of light and life in a lyrical manner. The fusion of precise detail and bold brushwork attains an artistic quality that transcends simple sketching. Viewers are initially struck by the butterfly but are soon drawn into the light in each water drop. It is an accomplished expression where life and frozen time intersect beautifully.
