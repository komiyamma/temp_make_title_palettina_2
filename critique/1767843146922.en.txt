1. Introduction
This painting depicts a mystical garden at dusk. Vibrant lights from colorful lanterns contrast with a white waterfall, creating a world where festive brilliance and meditative silence coexist. By expressing an oriental-themed garden through powerful brushstrokes and rich colors, the work invites viewers into an extraordinary space. It serves as an intriguing example for studying the interaction between artificial light and natural landscapes.

2. Description
On the left, a waterfall flows down layered rocks, its surface reflecting the surrounding lights. To the right stands a pavilion decorated with red fabrics and multicolored lanterns, housing golden vessels. In the foreground pond, two pale pink lotus flowers bloom among floating lily pads. A small stone lantern in the lower left emits a soft glow, illuminating nearby vegetation and adding to the scene's depth.

3. Analysis
The verticality of the waterfall and building intersects with the horizontal water surface, creating a stable composition. Particularly, the contrast between warm lantern light and cool night shadows gives the scene dramatic depth. Short, powerful brushstrokes prioritize the texture of light and color over sharp outlines.

4. Interpretation and Evaluation
The work symbolizes spiritual repose where festive elation and meditative stillness reside together. The lanterns represent human culture, while the waterfall and lotuses symbolize eternal nature; their fusion suggests harmony between humanity and the environment. The artist is commended for sublimating a complex night scene into a unified artwork through exceptional color sense. This piece successfully modernizes traditional motifs.

5. Conclusion
In conclusion, this painting presents an ideal aesthetic space through a magical fusion of light and color. While the initial impression is vivid, observation reveals a gentle harmony created by calculated gradations of light. This depiction, where artificial and natural beauty enhance each other, offers a new lyrical expression in landscape painting. The warm, mystical atmosphere brings profound peace and joy to the heart.
