This landscape painting depicts a grand historical building with a magnificent dome and its courtyard, viewed from a terrace in full bloom. In the right foreground, large roses with delicate petals and a rich presence form a solid foreground along with a stone balustrade. The viewer's gaze is led over these elements toward the palace-like structure emerging in the twilight.

Warm light spills from the building's windows, brilliantly illuminating its symmetrical facade. The figures of people crossing the square are rendered like stippling, suggesting the city's vitality and scale. The sky is a blend of pale purple and orange, evoking the magical hour at the end of the day. It is a work full of romanticism, successfully merging the still-life beauty of the foreground with the dynamic urban landscape in the distance.
