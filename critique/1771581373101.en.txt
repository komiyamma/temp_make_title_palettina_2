1. Introduction
This oil painting depicts a golden artifact abandoned on a sandy beach. The metallic texture and realistic shells immediately captivate the viewer. Within this fleeting moment of waves washing over the object lies a mysterious beauty suggesting a relic from eternity, imbuing the work with profound lyricism and convincing authority through its execution.

2. Description
A cylindrical brass object with intricate decorations lies in the center. Shells and pebbles surround it, with the sand's fine texture meticulously described. From the upper right, waves gently wash over the object, reflecting dazzling light onto the water. The palette harmonizes gold, brown sand, and transparent water, creating a warm yet quiet impression.

3. Analysis
Formally, the circular artifact and horizontal beach establish a stable composition. The brushwork is extremely careful, where micro-patterns on the metal and the damp feel of wet sand are rendered through precise touches. Light from above highlights metallic glints and sparkling water, creating sharp dimensionality and texture while strengthening the subject's overall presence.

4. Interpretation and Evaluation
The work interprets the "harmony between artificiality and nature" by contrasting manufactured metal with the sea and sand. The wave-washed relic evokes ancient glory, stimulating the imagination. Notably, the artist succeeds in differentiating textures like hard metal, fluid water, and varying states of sand, capturing quiet beauty through a unique aesthetic vision.

5. Conclusion
While the initial impression is dominated by dazzling realism, observation brings peace through the implied sound of tides. By casting light on a forgotten relic, a deep narrative emerges from beyond ordinary sight. In summary, this outstanding piece transforms a common seaside scene into a dramatic stage, possessing a universal beauty that transcends time.
