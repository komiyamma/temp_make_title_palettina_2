The color palette is dominated by warm reds and yellows, balanced by the cool purples of the flowers and the dark greens of the foliage. Light appears to originate from the upper right, casting subtle shadows that define the contours of the rooster窶冱 face. The composition is tightly cropped, which heightens the emotional intensity of the scene.
