1. Introduction
This composition presents a single sliced apple lying on a dark surface. The arrangement focuses on the physical qualities of the fruit. The creator examines the internal structure and the external texture of this natural subject. The straightforward presentation invites the observer to study the characteristics of the core and seeds.

2. Description
A half of a yellow and red apple rests horizontally in the frame. The exposed interior reveals a pale yellow flesh, a central core, and several dark brown seeds. The outer skin displays a mottled pattern of red and pale yellow hues. A distinct shadow falls to the right of the fruit.

3. Analysis
The visual structure relies on the central placement of the illuminated subject against a completely dark setting. The chromatic range contrasts the warm colors of the apple with the deep black of the background. A directional light source from the upper left highlights the moisture on the cut surface. This careful lighting creates a solid sense of volume.

4. Interpretation and Evaluation
The isolation of the sliced apple brings attention to its geometric internal structure. The detailed rendering of the seeds suggests an interest in the underlying design of organic forms. The composition functions like a formal scientific illustration combined with an atmospheric setting. The work successfully captures the physical state of the cut fruit.

5. Conclusion
This creation provides an observation of a botanical specimen. The deliberate contrast between the bright fruit and the dark surface highlights the subject effectively. The precise attention to lighting gives the apple a tangible reality. Ultimately, this work stands as a focused study of natural geometry.
