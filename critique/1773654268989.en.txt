This landscape painting depicts a Nordic-style village nestled along a fjord. In the right foreground, a prominent red wooden house with flowers in its window serves as a visual anchor. From there, the viewer's eye is led along the waterfront towards a cluster of houses, a church steeple, and the majestic mountains rising in the background. A slender waterfall cascading down the mountainside adds a dynamic element to the tranquil scene.

The work employs an impressionistic style, with delicate brushstrokes capturing the play of light on the water's surface. Atmospheric perspective is used effectively, with the distant mountains rendered in hazy blue tones to enhance the sense of depth. The vibrant contrast between the warm-colored buildings and the cool tones of the mountains and water creates a balanced and lyrical atmosphere. It is a work that captures a peaceful moment of coexistence between nature and humanity with assured technique.
