1. Introduction
This oil painting presents a poignant genre scene set in a rural farmyard at the close of the day. The composition focuses on a solitary female figure engaged in the routine task of tending to livestock. It captures a moment of quiet domesticity, reflecting the simple and hardworking life of the peasantry through a lens of warm, atmospheric realism.

2. Description
In the center, a woman wearing a white headscarf and a dark apron bends slightly as she feeds a group of chickens scattered at her feet. She holds a woven basket filled with white eggs in her left arm. To the left, the open door of a wooden barn reveals a faint internal glow, while the background features a sunset sky with silhouettes of trees and a haystacks.
