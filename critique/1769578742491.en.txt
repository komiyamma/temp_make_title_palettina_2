1. Introduction
This decorative oil painting depicts a red-haired woman preparing before a mirror, capturing a moment of private elegance. The composition centers on her back as she adjusts pearls in her hair, while her reflection reveals a focused expression. The work combines psychological depth with opulent light and texture.

2. Description
The subject sits with her back to the viewer, securing pearls within her styled red hair. She wears a magnificent crimson dress adorned with gold embroidery along the shoulders. A heavy, gilded mirror frame reflects her face with clarity. On the marble table, pearl necklaces, golden perfume bottles, and ornate jewelry boxes are arranged, adding a still-life quality to the scene.

3. Analysis
The powerful harmony of vibrant reds and golds creates a visual effect of nobility. Light from the upper left illuminates the woman窶冱 shoulders, highlighting the smooth texture of her skin and the sheen of her dress. The composition features a dual structure involving the physical subject and her mirrored image, creating depth. Meticulous rendering of objects demonstrates exceptional technical precision.

4. Interpretation and Evaluation
The painting explores the pursuit of beauty and the attempt to immortalize a fleeting moment. Its strength lies in the artist窶冱 ability to portray material textures alongside psychological interiority. The contrast between her hair and the pearls displays an original sensibility. Through dramatic lighting, the work achieves a compelling narrative quality.

5. Conclusion
Initially appearing as a display of opulence, analysis of the reflection reveals themes of self-reflection. This work represents a sophisticated fusion of technical mastery and intellectual composition. Ultimately, the painting invites the viewer to contemplate the essence of beauty and its representation in art.
