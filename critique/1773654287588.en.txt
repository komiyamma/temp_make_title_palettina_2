This landscape painting offers a view of Prague's Old Town from an elevated vantage point, possibly a window or a high terrace. In the center background, the distinctive twin spires of the Týn Church and the Old Town Hall Tower stand prominently as historical landmarks. The expanse of red-tiled roofs from the foreground to the middle ground deepens in color under the evening light, emphasizing the city's sprawl.

In terms of composition, the lush foliage on the left and the translucent curtain on the right act as a frame, enhancing the viewer's sense of immersion in the distant cityscape. The contrast between the golden-hued sky and the long shadows cast by the buildings evokes the tranquil atmosphere of the day's end. The combination of detailed architectural forms and emotional lighting perfectly conveys the unique poetic charm of this ancient Central European capital.
