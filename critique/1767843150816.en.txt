1. Introduction
This painting depicts a mountain stream flowing through a rugged range and a single, powerfully rooted pine tree. Using a muted palette and bold brushstrokes, it poetically expresses a scene where natural harshness and silence coexist. The mist provides depth and mystery, drawing the viewer into the pure mountain air. It serves as an excellent example for studying unified spatial expression through a limited color palette.

2. Description
From the upper left, a waterfall cascades down with white spray, leading to a stream winding through rocks. On the right stands an ancient pine tree with widely spreading branches, enduring the harsh environment on the rocky ground. Distant mountains are faintly depicted, shrouded in mist, while the sky is rendered in bright gray and white. The piece is unified by restrained earth colors, primarily browns and grayish-greens.

3. Analysis
The dynamic diagonal line from the waterfall to the stream is balanced by the verticality of the pine tree. High-value water and sky contrast with dark rocky surfaces and pine needles, establishing a distinct visual focus. Technically, the work makes frequent use of dry brushstrokes that reveal the canvas texture. This method effectively emphasizes the rugged rocks, the rough bark of the pine, and the energetic motion of the water.

4. Interpretation and Evaluation
The work symbolizes an "indomitable spirit" that sustains life within a harsh environment. The lone pine tree embodies strength against adversity, reinterpreting traditional symbolism through a modern style. The artist is commended for depicting nature's vitality solely through brushstroke momentum and light-dark composition without relying on flamboyant colors. The expressive power integrating classical dignity with bold technique is outstanding.

5. Conclusion
In conclusion, this work presents the harshness of nature and the sanctity of life with high artistic completion. While the initial impression is quiet, deeper observation reveals the overwhelming power of the forms created by vigorous brushstrokes. It is a fruition of the artist's sincere observational eye, capturing nature as it is. The sturdy beauty emitted by this work inspires quiet courage and profound respect for nature.
