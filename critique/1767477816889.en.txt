1. Introduction
The painting presents a vibrant coastal scene viewed from an elevated stone terrace. This composition captures a peaceful Mediterranean atmosphere, combining a lush floral foreground with a distant seaside town. The work is characterized by bright sunlight and a rich array of colors that evoke a sense of summer warmth.

2. Description
In the foreground, a round mosaic table holds a bouquet of flowers, accompanied by a single chair. Several terracotta pots filled with blooming flowers in red, yellow, and violet are arranged along the terrace. Beyond the stone balustrade, a hillside town with red-roofed buildings slopes toward the sea. White sailboats are scattered across the turquoise water.

3. Analysis
The artist employs energetic, broken brushstrokes that enhance the shimmering quality of the light and water. A brilliant color palette, featuring warm oranges and yellows contrasted against cool blues, creates a dynamic visual experience. The perspective is handled effectively, leading the viewer's eye from the terrace to the horizon. Light and shadow on the floor add a sense of depth.

4. Interpretation and Evaluation
This work celebrates the beauty of a coastal landscape and the tranquility of a sunny afternoon. The abundance of flowers and sailboats suggest a life of harmony with nature. The painter demonstrates technical skill in balancing floral details with broad landscape elements. The overall execution successfully conveys a joyful and inviting mood.

5. Conclusion
Initially, the vibrant colors of the flowers dominate the view, but the distant town eventually draws deeper interest. This painting serves as a window into a serene world, inviting the viewer to appreciate the scenery. It remains a compelling example of impressionistic landscape painting that captures the atmosphere of a specific place.
