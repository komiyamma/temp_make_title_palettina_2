1. Introduction
This painting captures a moment of diligent labor, focusing on a pair of weathered hands as they carefully polish a magnificent, patterned vase. The composition emphasizes the physical connection between the craftsman and the object, highlighting the tactile nature of maintenance and care. Through its rich textures and dramatic lighting, the work elevates a routine task into a scene of significant artistic merit.

3. Analysis
The artist employs a bold, painterly style that uses thick strokes to define the contours of the hands and the reflective surfaces of the metal.
