This landscape painting captures the iconic scenery of Santorini in the Aegean Sea. The central focus is a group of white churches with brilliant blue domes. The domes are arranged in an overlapping fashion from foreground to background, lending a sense of rhythm and depth to the composition. The bell tower on the left serves as a strong vertical axis, providing a stable contrast to the horizontal expanse of the sea.

The color palette features a perfect harmony of sky blue, deep sea blue, and the crisp white of the buildings. Splashes of pink bougainvillea placed near the corners of the frame act as vibrant accents, adding warmth and splendor to the predominantly cool-toned composition. The sunlight reflecting off the water's surface is rendered with delicate brushwork, effectively conveying the intense light characteristic of the Mediterranean. It is an excellent work that captures traditional architectural beauty and abundant natural light with a refreshing sense of color.
