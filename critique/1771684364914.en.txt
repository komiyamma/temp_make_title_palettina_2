1. Introduction
This oil painting captures a spirited scene in a bustling tavern, focusing on a cheerful woman raising a frothy mug. The work is a genre painting, celebrating the vibrant energy of social life and communal joy. Its lively atmosphere engages the viewer in the festive moment.

2. Description
The central figure is a woman with an exuberant smile, wearing a laced bodice, white blouse, and a red shawl. She holds a large stein overflowing with beer foam, her arm resting on a wooden table. The table holds a loaf of bread, grapes, and a flickering candle. In the blurred background, other patrons add to the sense of a crowded environment.

3. Analysis
