1. Introduction
The painting is a realistic oil work capturing a young woman focusing on a candle flame in a dark room. A brass lamp stands in the center, and the subject is depicted lighting a white candle. The contrast between the dark background and the illuminated subject creates a serene nocturnal atmosphere. Through chiaroscuro, the artist imbues this action with quiet contemplation.

2. Description
The central focus is a woman wearing a white headscarf and a reddish-brown dress. She gazes at the candle with a calm expression showing concentration. Her right hand holds a stick to ignite the flame, while her left hand rests on the lamp base. A pearl earring catches the light, and the textures of her chemise are carefully rendered. The brass lamp reflects the warm candlelight with metallic luster.

3. Analysis
The most prominent characteristic is the intense chiaroscuro, where the candle serves as the sole light source. This light vividly illuminates her face, neck, and chest, while casting the rest into deep shadow. The limited color palette harmonizes amber glows with the red dress and dark brown shadows. The brushwork distinguishes between the hard gleam of brass and the soft texture of skin.

4. Interpretation and Evaluation
This work explores the symbolic relationship between light and darkness. The area illuminated by the candle can be interpreted as a metaphor for hope or a silent prayer. The descriptive power goes beyond replicating details, embodying the sensation of stillness. The gaze and hand movements converge toward the lamp, achieving visual stability. The piece is regarded for its emotional expression and traditional techniques.

5. Conclusion
This painting elevates a quiet moment of daily life into art through the power of light. While the viewer is initially struck by the precision, deeper observation reveals spiritual tranquility. The woman watching the flame in the silence leaves a strong impression of peace and the preciousness of a fleeting moment. The work succeeds in embodying universal beauty through the fusion of technique and insight.
