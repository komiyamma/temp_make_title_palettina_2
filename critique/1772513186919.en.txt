1. Introduction
This painting is a striking portrait of a woman glancing over her shoulder while holding a fan. The composition utilizes a dynamic posture to create a strong sense of movement. Through bold contrasts, the image explores themes of fleeting beauty and theatrical elegance. The confident rendering establishes a highly memorable visual character.

2. Description
The central figure turns her head sharply to look back toward the viewer, wearing a black hat adorned with a white feather. Her attire consists of a dark dress accented with ruffled white fabric at the neckline. A single teardrop pearl earring is clearly visible. In her hand, she holds a bright folding fan featuring blue and red patterns.

3. Analysis
The artwork demonstrates exceptional handling of chiaroscuro, contrasting luminous skin tones against the dark clothing and background. Textural differences are carefully articulated, from the soft feather to the stiff folds of the fan. The color palette relies on deep blacks and brilliant whites, punctuated by vibrant accents. The curving line of the feather balances the shoulders.

4. Interpretation and Evaluation
The piece can be interpreted as a celebration of dramatic flair and individual confidence. The maker displays impressive technical skill in capturing the momentary nature of the backward glance, giving immediacy. The inclusion of the colorful fan serves as an anchor of visual interest. The execution reveals excellent control over structural design.

5. Conclusion
Through its dynamic pose and masterful use of contrast, the portrait delivers a captivating study of elegance. The initial impression of dramatic intensity evolves into an appreciation for the careful balance of light and color. This piece successfully combines theatrical presentation with meticulous technical proficiency.
