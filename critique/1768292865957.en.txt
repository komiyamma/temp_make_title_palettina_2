1. Introduction
This vertical oil painting presents a poetic evening scene featuring a young woman in traditional Japanese attire seated by a tranquil river. Set against the backdrop of a distant village illuminated by glowing lanterns, the composition captures a moment of quiet contemplation during the transition from sunset to night. The artist utilizes a vibrant impressionistic style to blend the human figure with the atmospheric elements of the landscape. It is a work that evokes a sense of nostalgia and the timeless beauty of a summer evening.

2. Description
In the foreground, a woman dressed in a light blue kimono adorned with pink floral patterns sits on a mossy rock, holding a round paper fan (uchiwa). She is shown in profile, her gaze directed toward the far bank where a row of traditional houses emits a warm, golden radiance. Several boats with glowing lanterns drift on the water, their reflections stretching across the ripples in bright orange and yellow streaks. In the sky above the distant mountains, a slender crescent moon hangs amidst the fading light of the sunset.
