2. Description
A blue wooden boat occupies the center, showing signs of long use. Thick ropes are coiled around it, with water dripping from its side to provide a vivid sense of immediacy. In the upper left, a setting sun dyes the rippling water golden, while distant boat shadows suggest a typical harbor scene blending activity and silence.

Texture differentiation and fluid water portrayal elevate this piece beyond a simple landscape into a powerful artistic statement.
