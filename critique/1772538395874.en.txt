1. Introduction
This still life piece features a light-reflecting polyhedron and spheres, rendered with bold brushstrokes and rich colors. The composition emphasizes the optical properties of the objects. Basic background information, such as the creator or date, cannot be confirmed. Educationally, the technique of reconstructing light through distinct brushwork holds significant value.

2. Description
A transparent polyhedron with numerous facets dominates the right side, reflecting surrounding light. Small spheres in bright shades of red, green, and blue are scattered near the center. A curved golden object appears on the left, while circular light particles float behind. These motifs are brilliantly illuminated against a dark background.

3. Analysis
Formal analysis reveals an attempt to materialize light by layering short brushstrokes. Red, green, blue, and yellow are used contrastingly, bringing dynamism to the composition. The transparent objects incorporate surrounding hues, creating an effect of internal illumination. Constructing forms through the juxtaposition of color planes, rather than outlines, enhances their optical qualities.

4. Interpretation and Evaluation
This work expresses the autonomous interplay of light and color through everyday objects. The depictive skill prioritizes striking visual impressions over strict realism. Placing the subjects across the entire frame immerses the viewer into the movement of light. Combining textured brushwork with bright colors offers a fresh perspective on material beauty.

5. Conclusion
The initial impression of a rough surface transforms into an understanding of a composition that captures the order of light. Finding rich colors in glass reflections stimulates the visual sensibility. By fusing material texture with light, this piece successfully demonstrates new possibilities in modern color representation.
