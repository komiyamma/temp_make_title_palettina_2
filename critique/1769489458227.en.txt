1. Introduction
This vertical landscape painting depicts a vibrant mountain stream cascading over a series of small, rocky waterfalls within a dense forest. The work focuses on the dynamic interplay between rushing water and the soft, golden light filtering through the overhead canopy. It captures a moment of natural energy and crystalline clarity, inviting the viewer into a secluded and refreshing woodland environment.

2. Description
In the foreground, the stream flows over mossy boulders, with small white wildflowers growing along the right bank. The central cascade catches the direct light, its white foam and spray rendered with bright, energetic brushstrokes. The background is filled with a lush green forest, where the sun窶冱 rays create a warm, golden glow against the leaves and the rising mist from the falling water.

3. Analysis
The composition is built around the diagonal and vertical movement of the water, which leads the eye from the foreground up toward the sunlit area in the background. The artist utilizes a rich palette of deep greens and earthy browns, punctuated by brilliant whites and golden yellows to represent the light. The textural contrast between the fluid, translucent water and the solid, textured rocks is meticulously achieved through layered oil techniques.

4. Interpretation and Evaluation
