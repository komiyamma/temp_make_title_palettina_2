1. Introduction
This oil painting captures a late autumn scene touched by early winter, featuring maple leaves, red berries, and glistening frost. Combining impressionistic brushwork with realistic details, the work exudes a unique sense of tension and vitality. Viewers are immediately struck by the color contrast emerging through the freezing atmosphere, experiencing both the harshness and beauty of nature. It is an ambitious piece that sharply captures the formal changes brought by minute weather shifts from an educational perspective.

2. Description
Near the center, several maple leaves are depicted with white frost clinging to their edges, while clusters of bright red berries on slender twigs appear behind them. The frost is rendered with sharp, crystal-like precision, conveying a direct sense of coldness that contrasts impressively with the warm hues of the foliage and fruit. Faded brown and yellow leaves are layered around them, while the background is treated in pale bluish-gray to make the foreground subjects stand out clearly.

3. Analysis
The formal strength of this piece lies in the fusion of impasto textural expression and the intricate rendering of frost. While the layering of paint creates a sense of volume for the leaves and soil, extremely fine strokes are used for the frost, effectively utilizing differences in visual resolution. By keeping the overall brightness low, the artist highlights the whiteness of the reflecting frost and the redness of the berries, creating a dramatic lighting effect. Diagonal branch movements provide a dynamic rhythm.

4. Interpretation and Evaluation
The artist expresses the resilience of life at the intersection of seasons using frost as a symbol of stillness. The leaves and berries, which retain their colors despite being frozen, imply the dignity of life enduring harsh environments. The combination of an impressionistic background and a hyper-realistic foreground is an original method that integrates subjective emotion with objective observation. This exceptional technical balance harmoniously unites contradictory elements within a single frame, representing high artistic value.

5. Conclusion
This painting is an outstanding work that portrays the delicate beauty hidden within harsh environments through original brushwork and color. While it initially appears to be a simple seasonal depiction, studying the individual frost crystals reveals the artist's sincere inquiry into the natural world. The colors, like flames of life lighting up the cold air, leave a deep impression and serve as an excellent educational tool for learning about nature's diverse expressions. It is a masterful piece reflecting the artist's refined sensitivity.
