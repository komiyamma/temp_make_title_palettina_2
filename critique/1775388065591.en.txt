1. Introduction
This landscape painting features vibrant hydrangeas and a vivid red temple structure. Through thick brushstrokes and striking color contrasts, the humid atmosphere of a rainy season in a traditional precinct is vividly captured. A wet stone stairway naturally guides the viewer's eye toward the sacred building.

2. Description
In the left foreground, blooming pink and purple hydrangeas are boldly arranged alongside a moss-covered stone lantern. A rain-soaked staircase leads up to a traditional temple painted in brilliant vermilion, featuring a mossy roof. Golden lanterns hang from the eaves, while white smoke rises from an incense burner placed near the entrance. The scene is framed by dense green foliage, with visible raindrops falling throughout.

3. Analysis
The artist effectively utilizes a thick impasto technique, noticeable in the texture of the hydrangeas and the reflective wet stone steps. This physically elevates the textures, adding three-dimensionality to the environment. The cool tones of the floral foreground and the intense warm red of the background temple create a dramatic contrast, emphasizing spatial depth. The rising smoke and falling rain introduce a gentle sense of movement.

4. Interpretation and Evaluation
This piece harmonizes seasonal changes with historic religious architecture. The glowing hydrangeas and weathered stones symbolize the vitality of the rainy season and enduring spirituality. The smoke and lit lanterns suggest human presence and continuous prayer. The artist's powerful brushwork and vivid colors dispel the gloominess of a rainy day, capturing its unique, striking beauty.

5. Conclusion
With a solid composition, this outstanding landscape poetically depicts a rain-soaked temple scene. From the glossy hydrangeas to the majestic temple, the passionate artistic expression leaves a profound and lingering visual impression.
