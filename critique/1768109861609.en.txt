1. Introduction
This oil painting, evoking early 19th-century Empire style, features a woman of neoclassical elegance. Reclining on a terrace overlooking a distant city, she embodies both aristocratic grace and intellectual stillness. The dominant golden light and rich colors create a world of historical weight and idealized beauty that powerfully impresses the viewer.

2. Description
A woman in a high-waisted white dress, pearls, and tiara reclines on a chaise lounge, holding a decorated fan. To her left, an ancient cityscape with a dome is visible beyond a massive Corinthian column. Red drapery and golden cushions surround her, while a brass candelabra sits on a nearby table.
