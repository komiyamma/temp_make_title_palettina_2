This landscape painting depicts a Mediterranean coastal town with characteristic white-washed buildings. A stone-stepped path running through the center of the composition draws the viewer's gaze down toward the sea in the distance. On the left, a white house with striking blue doors and shutters is framed by vibrant pink bougainvillea overhead, adding a splash of brilliance to the scene. Colorful potted flowers line the steps, imbuing the work with a sense of lived-in charm.

The bright sunlight is effectively conveyed through the sharp shadows cast on the white walls. The sea in the background is rendered in a vivid blue, accented by several white sailboats. Within the predominantly vertical and horizontal structure, the diagonal line of the steps creates a sense of depth and rhythmic space. The painting successfully blends the breezy atmosphere of Southern Europe with a well-ordered arrangement of color.
