1. Introduction
This oil painting presents a graceful portrait of a woman standing in a lush, sun-drenched Mediterranean courtyard. Surrounded by blooming flowers and a classical stone fountain, the subject exudes an air of timeless elegance. The setting provides a harmonious backdrop that complements the serene presence of the central figure.

2. Description
The woman is adorned in an exquisite off-the-shoulder lace gown, featuring intricate patterns that demonstrate remarkable detail. She holds a decorative folding fan against her torso while her other hand rests gently near her head. In the background, a double stone archway frames a distant building, while vibrant floral pots line the foreground.
