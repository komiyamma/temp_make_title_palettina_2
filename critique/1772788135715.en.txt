1. Introduction
This composition presents a fragmented still life scene constructed through intersecting geometric planes. The faceted approach breaks the subjects while maintaining their forms. This execution demonstrates a calculated study of everyday items seen from a specific geometric perspective.

2. Description
A glass containing golden liquid stands prominently in the left-central area of the composition. Behind it, a dark green bottle rests against the dynamic background structure. A cluster of dark red grapes lies near the base of the stem. A wedge of yellow cheese with circular holes occupies the lower right quadrant.

3. Analysis
The creator applies a multifaceted technique, breaking both the objects and the background into colored geometric patches. The palette contrasts deep blues and greens with warm oranges and yellows. Light refracts across the scene, creating an illuminated effect within the liquid and the cheese. Overlapping diagonal lines generate a rhythmic structure.

4. Interpretation and Evaluation
The arrangement transforms a traditional culinary spread into a complex study of dimension and light. The fractured surface acts as a shattered mirror reflecting scattered fragments of reality back to the viewer. This analytical approach encourages observers to perceive volume through flat segments. The work balances recognizable subject matter with rigorous geometric abstraction.

5. Conclusion
This creation offers a structured examination of still life elements through a fragmented visual vocabulary. The meticulous arrangement of color patches builds a dynamic representation of familiar objects. The synthesis of traditional subject matter and analytical geometric techniques results in a compelling visual study.
