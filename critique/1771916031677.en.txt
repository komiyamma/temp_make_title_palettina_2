1. Introduction
This oil painting depicts colorful wooden boats gathered in a harbor after nightfall. The water reflects golden shore lights, creating a fantastical scene. Viewers are struck by the powerful presence of boats in the foreground and the brilliant contrast of light. It is a high-quality work expressing both nocturnal tranquility and poetic vitality.

2. Description
In the foreground, boats in vibrant red, blue, and yellow overlap. The vessels contain ropes and cloths, rendered with textures suggesting years of use. Numerous boats are anchored into the distance, backed by lights glowing against the night sky. The water features fine ripples, transforming reflected light into scattered patterns.

3. Analysis
Complementary blue and orange tones form the primary palette, emphasizing visual brilliance. The brushwork is bold and dynamic, with visible impasto techniques. The depiction of light on the water uses short, layered strokes to create a shimmering atmosphere and volume. This dynamic approach imparts internal energy to the otherwise still scene.

4. Interpretation and Evaluation
The densely packed boats symbolize rest after labor and preparation for tomorrow. The warm lights evoke hope and community bonds, offering the viewer a sense of security. Blending traditional scenery with modern expressionist sensibility is highly commendable. This original piece represents a harmonious fusion of realistic observation and lyrical sentiment.

5. Conclusion
Through dramatic light and color, this painting breathes life into a universal theme. As one explores the work, the sound of waves and the swaying of boats become tangible. By transforming an everyday scene into a stage, the piece leaves a lasting impression. In conclusion, the work possesses significant artistic value, deeply captivating the audience.
