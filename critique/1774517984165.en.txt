1. Introduction
This painting features a surreal mountain scene composed of rainbow-colored strata. Vivid bands of color traversing the slopes sublimate a natural wonder into a kaleidoscopic visual experience. It is a work where realism and decorative beauty are fused, possessing the power to instantly invite the viewer into an extraordinary world.

2. Description
On the steep slopes, highly saturated bands of red, purple, turquoise, yellow, and orange run in nearly horizontal stripes. Out-of-focus golden grass in the foreground provides depth. Along the middle-ground ridges, tiny hikers are scattered, emphasizing the scale of the massive mountain. A pale blue sky with thin clouds covers the top.

3. Analysis
The sharp V-shaped ridge and corresponding regular strata lines give the screen a powerful rhythm. While based on reality, the exaggerated colors create an illusory atmosphere. Intentionally blurring the foreground grass directs the eye to the colorful subject, effectively using a photographic technique to focus the viewer's attention.

4. Interpretation and Evaluation
This work expresses geological mysteries as a torrent of life-filled energy. Highlighting natural details through an extreme choice of colors, the piece redefines the artistry inherent in the Earth. The overlapping fine lines and exquisite color transitions demonstrate the author's extraordinary color sense and stable descriptive technique.

5. Conclusion
This work succeeds in depicting both the grandeur and delicacy of nature through color. Initially, one is overwhelmed by the intense hues; however, noticing the tiny humans allows a deeper understanding of nature's majesty and the dignity of human activity. It is an original masterpiece that expands the possibilities of landscape art.
