1. Introduction
This painting depicts a dramatic coastal landscape bathed in the intense light of sunset. A stone pier jutting into a turbulent sea and a distant lighthouse serve as the primary motifs. A wooden post wrapped in thick rope heavily dominates the foreground. The extreme perspective and theatrical lighting amplify the raw power of nature.

2. Description
A wet stone pier cuts through the center of the canvas toward the horizon. At its vanishing point, the silhouette of an illuminated lighthouse emerges. White waves crash violently against the pier. The sky exhibits a rich gradient from vibrant orange to dark gray. In the foreground, the textures of weathered wood and rough rope are detailed.

3. Analysis
The most striking feature is the textured surface created by a heavy impasto technique. This thick application of paint visually communicates the hardness of the stones and splashing waves. The strong contrast between the warm light and deep shadows infuses the composition with dramatic tension. The depth established by the dark foreground pillar and the bright lighthouse is quite effective. 

4. Interpretation and Evaluation
This artwork successfully captures the sublime beauty of light and the resilience of structures in a harsh environment. The radiant lighthouse and its reflection can be interpreted as a symbol of hope. The emotional resonance generated by the rough brushwork and intense color contrasts is highly evaluated. It demonstrates the painter's solid technical skills.

5. Conclusion
Through bold brushstrokes, this painting elevates a coastal scene into a profound visual experience. The initial impression of a simple landscape deepens into a moving atmospheric space. It is a striking work that unites intense materiality with masterful composition.
