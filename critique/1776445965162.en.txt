1. Introduction
This oil painting presents a dynamic depiction of a coastal city transitioning from dusk to night, executed with a vigorous impasto technique. A majestic mountain dominates the central composition, while the warm glow of city lights spreads across its base and along the shoreline, contrasting sharply against the deep purple landscape. The viewer is positioned at an elevated vantage point, looking through foreground branches that frame this expansive panorama and establish a sense of depth.

2. Description
The lower and left foreground areas feature dark, heavily textured rocks and vegetation that provide a solid foundation for the scene. In the middle ground, the steep silhouette of a mountain rises, dotted with concentrated clusters of golden light that represent buildings and streets. These lights extend toward the waterfront, where their reflections stretch across the dark sea in shimmering golden ribbons. The sky above transitions from a vibrant orange near the horizon to a cool, layered violet.

3. Analysis
The most striking formal element is the painterly texture created by thick palette knife strokes, which lend the canvas a sculptural quality. Every surface, from the craggy mountain to the rolling clouds, is defined by these bold applications of pigment that catch and refract ambient light. Coloristically, the work relies on the powerful interplay between complementary deep purples and radiant oranges, effectively emphasizing the contrast between the cool night air and the warmth of human habitation.

4. Interpretation and Evaluation
This work achieves originality by reconstructing a traditional landscape motif through an improvisational and expressive shorthand. By prioritizing the mass of light and color over literal detail, the artist successfully captures the atmospheric essence of the night. The composition is well-balanced, and the rhythmic distribution of color—particularly in the water's reflections—demonstrates a sophisticated mastery of the medium and an exceptional sense of harmony.

5. Conclusion
While the initial impression is one of raw energy and heavy application, closer inspection reveals a carefully orchestrated spatial structure defined by light. The painting serves as a powerful affirmation of the hidden colors within the darkness and the vibrant pulse of life within a serene natural setting.
