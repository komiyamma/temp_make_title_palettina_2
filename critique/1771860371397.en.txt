1. Introduction
This painting presents a lyrical depiction of maple leaves covered in frost, capturing the poignant transition between seasons. Vibrant autumn colors contrasted with the cool white light of winter dominate the composition. Heavy impasto techniques recreate the physical texture of snow against burning reds, symbolizing nature's harsh beauty and representing a successful endeavor to merge traditional themes with rich, tactile texture.

2. Description
On the right, several maple leaves transitioning from orange to deep red are arranged in overlapping layers. Their edges are adorned with thick white paint, representing frost with tangible volume. On the left and in the background, withered branches and bark are rendered in cool bluish-grays and browns, accentuating the foreground窶冱 warm tones. The background brushwork is abstracted, creating visual distance from the primary subject.
