2. Description
The central figure is a woman in profile, kneeling gracefully in a green and gold patterned kimono. She carefully adjusts a bouquet of pink and white peonies held in a large, ornate golden vase. In the foreground, a small ceramic incense burner releases a delicate trail of white smoke, while the background consists of a gold-leaf screen decorated with faint floral motifs.

4. Interpretation and Evaluation
The work explores themes of transience and permanence through the juxtaposition of the fleeting smoke, the wilting beauty of the flowers, and the enduring luster of the golden screen. The artist displays a masterful ability to render metallic surfaces and complex textiles. This piece is a highly successful marriage of formal decorative beauty and a deeper philosophical reflection on the appreciation of the moment.
