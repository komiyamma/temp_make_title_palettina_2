1. Introduction
This work is a vibrant oil-style painting depicting a bustling flower market illuminated by colorful lanterns at night. The abundance of blossoms filling the canvas, combined with the warm glow of lights, creates a festive atmosphere. Powerful brushwork and vivid colors successfully capture the market's energy and vitality, resulting in a visually impactful piece that celebrates life in a urban setting.

2. Description
From the left to the center, a diverse array of flowers overflows from baskets and buckets. Sunflowers and purple blossoms stand in the foreground, while red roses stretch into the background. Overhead, rows of red and yellow lanterns cast reflections onto the wet pavement. Three small figures walk toward the viewer in the distance, adding a human element, while warm light glows from a distant building.

3. Analysis
Warm tones like red, yellow, and orange dominate the palette, creating dynamism paired with complementary purples and greens. The brushwork uses thick impasto, where each stroke functions as an independent block of color, reminiscent of Expressionism. Reflections on the ground use fragmented color planes to represent wet textures and diffused light. A perspective-based composition guides the gaze from the detailed flowers to the distant figures.

4. Interpretation and Evaluation
The work affirms the festivity of daily life through a dance of light and color. Artificial lantern light enhancing natural hues produces a unique lyricism blending urbanity with natural beauty. Bold color expression goes beyond recording a scene, directly appealing to the viewer's emotions. The sharp sense of color, maintaining high saturation while achieving overall harmony, is particularly commendable and demonstrates the artist's technical maturity.

5. Conclusion
This painting breathes new life into the theme of a nocturnal market through light and rich coloration. The density of information and passionate brushwork provide the viewer with bright vitality. While initially captivated by the torrent of color, one eventually discovers the delicate shadows and the beauty reflected on the ground. This piece serves as a visual anthem, expressing the profound joy that light brings to the world.
