1. Introduction
This powerful figurative painting depicts a stone sculptor intensely focused on his craft within a workshop. The work utilizes a robust style that emphasizes the physical labor and dedication involved in traditional sculpture. It serves as an exploration of the creative process and the raw strength required to transform unyielding stone into art.

2. Description
A muscular male figure dominates the composition, leaning over a large block of stone with a hammer and chisel. He wears a simple tunic and a red sash. In the background, a finished statue stands in shadow, while an archway reveals a distant city skyline with a dome under a bright sky.
