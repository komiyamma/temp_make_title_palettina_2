1. Introduction
This oil painting presents a breathtaking coastal landscape, capturing a moment of serene transition between land and sea. The artist focuses on a rocky shoreline where large, weathered boulders meet the restless ocean, all under the soft glow of a setting or rising sun. The presence of a solitary lighthouse on the distant horizon adds a sense of narrative and human scale to the vast natural environment.

2. Description
The composition is dominated by massive reddish-brown rocks in the foreground, their surfaces textured with intricate patterns and shadows. In the bottom left corner, clusters of small purple flowers emerge from the crevices, providing a delicate contrast to the rugged stone. The sea, rendered in shades of turquoise and emerald, crashes against the rocks, resulting in dynamic sprays of white foam. In the upper right background, a tall stone lighthouse stands atop a rocky outcrop against a pale, cloud-streaked sky.

3. Analysis
Lighting plays a crucial role in this work, with warm, directional light highlighting the craggy edges of the rocks and the crests of the waves. The brushwork is expressive and varied, using thick impasto for the foam and more blended strokes for the sky. The color palette creates a strong atmospheric quality, balancing the warm tones of the sun-drenched granite with the cool, translucent hues of the seawater. Atmospheric perspective is effectively used to create depth, as the distant cliffs and lighthouse appear softer in detail.

4. Interpretation and Evaluation
The painting successfully evokes a sense of enduring strength and the rhythmic power of nature. The juxtaposition of the permanent stone, the fleeting light, and the delicate flora suggests a complex ecological balance. The technical execution is exceptional, particularly in the artist's ability to render the translucency of water and the tactile quality of the weathered rocks. The composition guides the viewer's eye skillfully from the intimate foreground details to the expansive horizon beyond.

5. Conclusion
In summary, this work is a masterful exploration of light and texture within a classic maritime theme. The painting invites viewers to contemplate the intersection of the wild coastline and the quiet presence of the lighthouse. Through careful observation and expressive technique, the artist has transformed a familiar coastal scene into a compelling study of natural beauty. The final impression is one of profound tranquility combined with the untamed energy of the sea.
