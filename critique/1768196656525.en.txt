1. Introduction
This vertical oil painting captures a charming genre scene of two young children interacting with nature beside an old stone fountain. The work depicts a moment of innocent generosity, as the children share their bread with a group of ducks. It serves as an exploration of light, youthful expression, and the harmonious relationship between humans and animals within a rural setting.

2. Description
A young girl with long, curly hair and a red headscarf sits on a stone ledge, holding a wicker basket filled with bread. To her right, a young boy in a simple white shirt stands barefoot, carefully breaking a piece of bread to throw into the water. Below them, five ducks of varying plumage gather in a shallow, sun-flecked pool, eagerly awaiting the food. Behind the children, water flows from a rustic stone spout into the basin, framed by lush green foliage and a clear, bright sky.
