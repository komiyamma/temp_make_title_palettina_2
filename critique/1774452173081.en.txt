1. Introduction
This watercolor on paper captures a serene and evocative river scene set within a dramatic karst mountain landscape. The composition focuses on a central waterway where a fisherman on a bamboo raft navigates towards the viewer, surrounded by towering, limestone peaks that recede into a soft, atmospheric distance. This work serves as an excellent example of how the watercolor medium can effectively depict the interplay of light, water, and rugged geological forms through translucent washes and delicate brushwork.

2. Description
In the middle ground, a bamboo raft carries a fisherman in a conical hat, accompanied by several small animals, likely cormorants, and a basket. A second, smaller raft is visible further upriver to the left, beneath the shadow of distant, hazy blue mountains. To the right, a massive, sheer cliff face rises vertically, its sunlit surfaces rendered in warm ochres and pale yellows, while its shadowed crevices are filled with lush green foliage. The foreground is framed by dark, overhanging bamboo leaves in the upper left and tall, linear grasses at the bottom left, anchoring the viewer's perspective.

3. Analysis
The artist expertly utilizes the transparency of watercolor to create a luminous effect, particularly in the reflections on the river's surface, which mirror the warm highlights of the mountain. The use of a "wet-on-wet" technique for the distant peaks creates a convincing sense of atmospheric perspective and depth. Contrast is achieved through the sharp, fine lines of the raft and figures against the soft, blended tones of the water and sky. The vertical orientation of the piece emphasizes the height of the karst formations, while the diagonal flow of the river leads the eye smoothly through the composition.

4. Interpretation and Evaluation
This painting successfully conveys a sense of timelessness and harmony between human activity and the natural environment. The technical execution is exceptional, showing a mastery of both broad, expressive washes and controlled, detailed descriptive elements. The artist's ability to balance the warm and cool color palettes creates a vibrant yet tranquil atmosphere that highly engages the viewer. While the subject matter is classic, the confident handling of the watercolor medium and the sophisticated composition elevate the work to a high level of artistic achievement.

5. Conclusion
An initial impression of tranquil beauty matures into an appreciation for the artist's technical skill in capturing the specific qualities of light and atmosphere in this unique landscape. The painting beautifully balances the solid permanence of the limestone cliffs with the fluid, ever-changing nature of the river. Its success lies in its ability to evoke a powerful sense of place and a feeling of quiet contemplation. Ultimately, the work remains a captivating exploration of light and form, leaving the viewer with a profound sense of the enduring grace found in nature.
