1. Introduction
This bright and airy oil painting captures a serene moment of a young woman adjusting her hair within a sun-drenched, natural setting. The work employs a delicate impressionistic style that emphasizes the play of light and the softness of the atmosphere, evoking a sense of youthful elegance and quiet joy.

2. Description
The subject is a woman in profile, dressed in a light blue kimono adorned with delicate pink and white floral patterns. She is shown with both hands raised to her dark, wavy hair, which is decorated with large, intricate floral ornaments in white and pink. The background is a vibrant tapestry of greenery and sunlight, with dappled light filtering through the leaves and illuminating the scene with a warm, golden glow.
