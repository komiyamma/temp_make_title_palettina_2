2. Description
A paint-stained hand grips a brush, blending vibrant yellow paint on the palette. The surface is piled with colors, including red, blue, green, and white, each arranged with a substantial texture. The radiance of the central yellow is emphasized through contrast with surrounding cool and neutral tones. The mounds of paint show complex tonal shifts as they merge, each cluster possessing its own subtle shadows. Diagonal lines created by the brush and hand introduce rhythmic motion and depth. The impasto technique creates a sculptural surface that reflects light in multiple directions, which is a significant formal characteristic of the piece.
