1. Introduction
This vertical painting presents a romanticized view of Venice during a radiant sunset, focusing on a woman in a flowing white dress boarding a gondola. The work captures the iconic architectural landmarks of the city alongside a moment of elegant daily life on the Grand Canal.

2. Description
In the foreground, a young woman steps into a gondola filled with vibrant flowers, assisted by a man in traditional attire. To the left, a grand palace with arched loggias and a tall campanile rises into the sky, while the midground is crowded with numerous gondolas and sailing vessels. The background features the distinctive domes of a basilica against a dramatic, cloud-filled sky illuminated by the setting sun, which reflects brilliantly across the rippling water.

3. Analysis

4. Interpretation and Evaluation
The painting evokes a sense of nostalgia and timeless beauty, celebrating the unique charm of Venetian maritime culture.
