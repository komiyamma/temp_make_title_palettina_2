The work reflects the aesthetic traditions of 19th-century academic realism, characterized by its meticulous attention to detail and romanticized atmosphere. She wears an elaborate gown featuring a deep red bodice with gold embroidery, complemented by voluminous white lace sleeves and a vibrant blue shawl draped over her arm. In her hands, she cradles a small bouquet of wildflowers, including daisies and pink blossoms, which echo the flora visible in the garden outside the window.
