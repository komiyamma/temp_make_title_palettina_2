1. Introduction
The current work presents a classic still life composition arranged on a wooden table. A skull, an apple, a clear glass, bound books, and a blue vase holding bright yellow sunflowers populate the scene. The creator balances themes of life and mortality within a unified visual framework.

2. Description
A striking contrast emerges between the lively botanical elements and the macabre focal point. Luminous sunflowers extend upward with vivid yellow petals and textured brown centers. Beside the blossoms lies a weathered skull, a red apple, and thick books with worn covers. A clear vessel reflects subtle highlights, grounding the arrangement.

3. Analysis
The composition utilizes chiaroscuro to guide the eye across the assorted objects. Shadows obscure the background, pushing illuminated items forward. Warm yellow hues stand out against the cool blue container and the muted table. This chromatic scheme creates a dynamic tension between the distinct components of the display.

4. Interpretation and Evaluation
The deliberate grouping functions as a memento mori, reminding observers of the fleeting nature of existence. The blooming flowers represent vitality, while the skull serves as a stark emblem of decay. The accompanying books suggest the limits of human knowledge. Ultimately, the piece prompts reflection on the balance between flourishing life and ultimate demise.

5. Conclusion
This visual arrangement merges botanical beauty with existential themes. The meticulous lighting and chosen objects create an engaging experience. By juxtaposing sunflowers with a symbol of death, the creator delivers a powerful statement. The composition remains a compelling example of traditional still life symbolism.
