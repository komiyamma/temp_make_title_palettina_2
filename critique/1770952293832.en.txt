1. Introduction
This painting depicts a solitary white egret standing in a mountain stream during late autumn. The artist captures a profound sense of stillness, using vibrant autumn leaves to frame the pristine white subject. This composition evokes a moment of suspended animation, where the flow of nature meets an eternal, silent presence.

2. Description
The egret occupies the center, its neck curved gracefully as it gazes toward the water. A single droplet hangs from its beak, introducing subtle tension to the frozen scene. The background consists of a cascading stream over rugged rocks, with red and orange maple leaves hanging from above. Scattered leaves around the bird窶冱 feet emphasize the late season.

The color palette relies on a contrast between the warm foliage and the cool tones of the stream. The yellow eye serves as a focal point, drawing attention to its lifelike gaze.
