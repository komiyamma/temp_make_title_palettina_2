2. Description
A broad, calm blue lake stretches beautifully across the expansive middle ground, flawlessly reflecting the clear morning sky above. On the immediate near shore, a vibrant red wooden gate stands prominently beside the gentle water edge. Lush pink cherry blossoms gracefully frame the top and left sides of the viewing area, adding a soft, pleasant floral texture.

Bright natural light falls gently on the rugged mountain peak, seamlessly drawing the viewer eye directly toward the distant center. The clever framing effect of the blooming floral branches establishes a remarkably strong sense of visual depth.
