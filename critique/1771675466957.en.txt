1. Introduction
This oil painting is a compelling portrait of a woman with vibrant red hair, executed in a style that evokes the dramatic flair of the Baroque period. The subject is depicted from a three-quarter rear view, looking back at the viewer with an intense and enigmatic expression. Holding a decorative feather fan, she is framed by a dark, architectural archway that enhances the theatrical atmosphere of the work.

The contrast between the soft, airy texture of the feathers and the heavy, metallic sheen of the shawl adds tactile complexity to the visual experience.

4. Interpretation and Evaluation
This portrait can be interpreted as a study of personality and social poise.
