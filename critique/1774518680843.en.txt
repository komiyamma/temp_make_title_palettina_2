1. Introduction
This painting depicts a frozen lake or sea at sunrise or sunset in a sub-zero region. The composition, where an icy world bathed in soft light spreads beyond dark rocky walls, presents a unique worldview of silence and beauty. It is a work that captures delicate changes of light woven by nature with expressive brushstrokes.

2. Description
On the left, a dark rock surface enveloped in deep shadows is prominently positioned. From the center to the right, cracked ice floes and snow-covered rocks are scattered across clear blue water. Soft orange and pink clouds fill the sky, warmly illuminating the edges of the ice and the reflection on the water's surface.

3. Analysis
The artwork is characterized by intentional brushwork reminiscent of Impressionism, utilizing paint texture to express the tactile feel of snow and ice. The contrast between saturated cool and warm colors gives a vivid brilliance to the scene. Through a composition emphasizing brightness differences, the heavy texture of the foreground and the light atmosphere of the background are clearly distinguished.

4. Interpretation and Evaluation
This work emotionally expresses the blessing of light in a severe environment. By combining contrasting materials—solid rock walls symbolizing permanence and fragile ice reflecting shifting light—the harmony between movement and stillness is portrayed. The direction of temperature through color and the technique emphasizing materiality are highly evaluated as original expressions.

5. Conclusion
Through the drama of light and texture, this work conveys the vitality and serene beauty of extreme environments. At first, it felt like a simple winter landscape; however, by tracing the light in each brushstroke, one could rediscover the warmth and mystery in a frozen world. The poetic sentiment leaves a deep resonance in the observer's heart.
