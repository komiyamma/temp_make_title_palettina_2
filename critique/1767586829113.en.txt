2. Description
A young woman stands at the center of the composition, holding a palette and brush as she works on a painting of sunflowers. To her left, a large, multi-paned window allows bright daylight to flood the room, illuminating a vase of real sunflowers on a nearby table. Several other finished and unfinished canvases are visible in the background, creating a layered environment of art. In the lower right, a ginger cat rests comfortably on a patterned armchair, while the surrounding surfaces are scattered with paint jars and brushes.

3. Analysis
The composition is vertically oriented, with the window and the artist forming a central axis that balances the dense, active interior. The artist employs a post-impressionist style, characterized by short, rhythmic brushstrokes and a bright, saturated palette dominated by vibrant yellows, blues, and oranges. The intense light from the window creates high-key highlights and soft shadows, blending the boundaries between the physical studio environment and the art being produced within it. This technique creates a unified, vibrating atmosphere.
