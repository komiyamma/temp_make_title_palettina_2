1. Introduction
This painting depicts a classic tram running through a dimly lit street wet with rain. Heavy European-style architecture lines both sides, while a hazy spire stands in the background. Rough, powerful brushstrokes dominate the canvas, making the warm light reflecting on wet cobblestones highly impressive. The city's hustle and the lyrical atmosphere of a rainy night are skillfully expressed.

2. Description
In the center, a red and yellow tram advances toward the foreground along a curving track. Its headlights and interior lights illuminate the surroundings, creating bright golden reflections on the wet cobblestones and rails. On both sides, silhouettes of people walking with umbrellas are depicted. Overhead wires intersect, and a massive church-like structure emerges vaguely against a blue-gray sky.

3. Analysis
The greatest appeal lies in the visual impact of the thick impasto and the brilliant expression of light reflection. In contrast to the cool tones of the sky, warm orange and yellow hues dominate the lower areas. This color contrast creates a dramatic atmosphere. The curved rails form a strong perspective, emphasizing the tram's dynamic forward motion.

4. Interpretation and Evaluation
This artwork successfully transforms a rainy street into a romantic, extraordinary space. The artificial lights glowing in the cold rain can be interpreted as a symbol of human warmth and urban vitality. The painter's ability to capture atmospheric moisture and flickering light through rough touches is extremely sharp. The spatial composition and exceptional color sense are highly evaluated.

5. Conclusion
By combining bold brushwork with dramatic lighting, this painting captures the rainy cityscape with rich emotion. It directly conveys the dynamic energy of the city and a deeply nostalgic atmosphere to the viewer. It is an immersive masterpiece that wonderfully harmonizes vibrant urban life with the poetic tranquility of rain.
