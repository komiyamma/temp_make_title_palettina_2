1. Introduction
This vertical composition presents a majestic view of the Arc de Triomphe in Paris, captured under the radiant glow of afternoon sunlight. The painting focuses on the architectural grandeur of the monument, which stands as a powerful symbol of French history and national identity. The presence of a large, draped French flag adds a dynamic and patriotic element to the historic scene.

2. Description
The stone surface of the arch is rendered with heavy impasto, highlighting the intricate relief sculptures and the weathered texture of the masonry. On the left, a portion of the tricolore flag hangs prominently, its colors contrasting with the warm ochre tones of the stone. In the foreground, an elegant black street lamp and lush green foliage frame the scene, while small, impressionistic figures gather at the base.

3. Analysis
The artist employs a thick application of paint to create a palpable sense of light and shadow on the monument’s facade. The central archway acts as a dark anchor that draws the viewer’s eye inward, while the golden sunlight illuminates the upper sections with brilliant intensity. The vertical orientation emphasizes the monumental scale of the structure, further enhanced by the low-angle perspective.

4. Interpretation and Evaluation
The work effectively communicates a sense of enduring strength and national pride through its bold brushwork and vibrant color palette. The interplay between the static architectural form and the fluid motion of the flag creates a lively tension within the frame. The technical execution of the stone’s texture and the atmospheric lighting demonstrates a high degree of artistic skill and a deep appreciation for classical urban subjects.

5. Conclusion
What begins as a familiar depiction of a Parisian landmark evolves into a profound study of how light interacts with history and stone. The painting captures the singular spirit of a grand metropolis during a moment of brilliant clarity. It serves as a compelling tribute to the architectural heritage of Paris and the vibrant energy of its public life.
