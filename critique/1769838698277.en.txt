1. Introduction
This evocative genre painting depicts a young woman in rustic attire carefully arranging a basket of daisies and wildflowers. Set against a warm, sun-drenched background, the work captures a quiet moment of rural life with exceptional grace. It is a masterful study of light and character, blending naturalistic detail with a soft, idealized atmosphere that recalls the academic traditions of the nineteenth century.

2. Description
The central figure, a woman with a cream-colored headscarf and a modest blouse, leans over a woven wicker basket overflowing with white daisies and small blue flowers. Her expression is one of focused tranquility as her hands gently touch the delicate petals. Light pours in from the upper left, illuminating her face and the intricate textures of her clothing, while the background remains a soft, blurred suggeston of a wooden structure or forest.
