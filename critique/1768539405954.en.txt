2. Description
At the center, an easel holds a coastal landscape depicting blue waters and rugged cliffs. The foreground is filled with the tools of the trade: a jar of brushes, paint tubes, and a palette covered in a kaleidoscope of colors. To the left stands a classical plaster bust, while several unfinished canvases lean against the back wall. An ornate lamp hangs from the ceiling, bathing the room in a warm, golden light that highlights the various textures.

3. Analysis
The color palette combines vivid primary colors from the palette with the warm, earthy tones of the studio interior. The artist employs a bold impasto technique, using thick strokes to give physical weight to the objects and create a sense of atmosphere. The composition follows a zigzag path that leads the viewer窶冱 eye through the scattered tools toward the back of the room. The lighting is focused, creating dramatic highlights and deep shadows that add to the room窶冱 depth.
