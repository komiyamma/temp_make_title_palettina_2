1. Introduction
This work is an interior painting depicting a woman arranging flowers. In a space filled with soft natural light, a modest daily activity is captured in a lyrical manner. The delicate expression of light perfectly conveys a tranquil atmosphere. It serves as an excellent educational example of combining figures with still life and rendering daylight indoors.

2. Description
On the left, a woman in a white ruffled dress arranges purple flowers in a glass vase. The transparent vase holds a rich arrangement including pink roses. In the background, a large window receives bright sunlight. A patterned cloth covers the table, where several cut flowers and leaves are scattered loosely.

3. Analysis
Formal analysis reveals the treatment of light is highly impressionistic. Sunlight streaming through the window falls on the dress and flowers, creating complex shadows. Regarding color composition, the pure white dress contrasts vividly with the purple and pink blooms, adding brilliant hues. The woman's arms guide the gaze toward the central vase.

4. Interpretation and Evaluation
This work celebrates the harmony between natural beauty and tranquil human life. The outstanding depiction skills reproduce the texture of clear glass and soft fabric through precise brushwork. The impressive rendering of light and skillful placement of colors elevate a daily scene into a rich experience. The spatial expression is highly valued.

5. Conclusion
Through masterful lighting, this piece elevates an ordinary scene into a beautiful creative work. While initially captivated by vibrant flowers, the viewer notices the meticulous representation of light reflections and textures. By portraying the interplay between elegant gestures and nature with excellent technique, this painting brings profound peace to the observer.
