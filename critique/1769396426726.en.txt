1. Introduction
This interior painting offers a warm and detailed view of a rustic, traditional kitchen bathed in natural sunlight. The work employs a descriptive and painterly style that emphasizes the textures of everyday objects and the inviting atmosphere of a domestic space. The composition highlights the abundance of fresh produce and the historic character of the kitchen窶冱 architecture and furnishings.

2. Description
The scene is centered on a heavy wooden table laden with baskets of carrots, onions, and various ceramic dishes. To the left, a large wooden cupboard stands filled with earthenware, while copper pots hang prominently from the ceiling over a stone cooking area. A large window and an open door on the right allow golden sunlight to flood the tiled floor, revealing a glimpse of a lush, sunlit garden just beyond the threshold.

3. Analysis
The artist uses a warm, earth-toned palette dominated by ochres, browns, and the metallic gleam of copper. A strong directional light source from the right creates long shadows and brilliant highlights, enhancing the three-dimensional quality of the various kitchen implements. The brushwork is expressive yet controlled, successfully capturing the distinct textures of the rough-hewn wood, the smooth ceramic glazes, and the varied surfaces of the fresh vegetables.
